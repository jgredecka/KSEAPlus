Choosing the Database

Data quality may vary depending on the kinase-substrate database used. Our analysis shows that PDTS and EDGES databases contain a higher proportion of kinases associated with a substrate count (SC) of at least 5 when compared to PSP, as summarised in the table below. However, PhosphoSitePlus database is also known to contain only direct kinase-substrate relationships, as opposed to PDTS and EDGES where substrate targets can be both direct as well as downstream targets.

Unique K-S links Total unique kinases (SC >= 1) Total unique kinases (SC >= 5) % Total unique kinases (SC >= 5)
PSP 10411 359 225 63
PDTS 19381 105 88 84
EDGES 16648 88 83 94
